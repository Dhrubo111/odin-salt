Rock Salt

Rock Salt occurs after the evaporation of salt water bodies, mainly lakes.
As a result, we are left with the crystalized texture and there are different forms.
Rock salt has uses in home remedies. One of them is to gargle a rock salt and water mixture to combat a sore throat.
